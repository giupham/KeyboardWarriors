He sat for a while very sorrowfully, then suddenly he felt in his pocket and
found his tobacco pipe, which was still half full. ‘This shall be my last
pleasure,’ thought he, pulled it out, lit it at the blue light and began to
smoke. When the smoke had circled about the cavern, suddenly a little black
dwarf stood before him, and said: ‘Lord, what are your commands?’ ‘What my
commands are?’ replied the soldier, quite astonished. ‘I must do everything
you bid me,’ said the little man. ‘Good,’ said the soldier; ‘then in the first
place help me out of this well.’
